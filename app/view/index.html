Chingaza National Park Bird Image Classifier

Upload your photos taken within the Chingaza National Park of Colombia to identify over 200 bird species!
Please note: This classifier was specifically trained on birds with a maximum altitude range greater than 2700m (e.g. as found within the Andean Humid Forest areas)
As such, not all species found within the Chingaza National Park are covered. Therefore, should the classification be incorrect, please email mikefullergm@gmail.com with the image.
